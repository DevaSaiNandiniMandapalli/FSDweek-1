Easy Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together the butter and sugar.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Gradually add the flour and milk, alternating between the two, and mix until smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Remove from the oven and let cool before serving.

Cooking Terms:

Cream together
Mixing softened butter and sugar until smooth and creamy.
Gradually
Adding a small amount at a time, slowly incorporating into the mixture.
